About This Website

Welcome to our website! This platform is designed to provide an interactive map experience and analysis tools for various topics in Nepal. The website features four pages, each serving a unique purpose:

1. Tourist Map of Nepal

The first page of this website is dedicated to showcasing the tourist map of Nepal. It includes three interactive maps that provide information about various services and locations in Nepal.

  • Banks (WMS)
  • ATM (WMS)

  • Baby Sitting

2. Rural School Analysis

The second page of the website is focused on rural school analysis. It features a map of Nepal showing the boundary, rural districts, roads, and schools, with two types of analysis:

  • Nearest Road to School: When a school is clicked, the nearest roads within 1 km distance are shown on the map.
  • Nearest School to Road: When a road is clicked, the nearest school and its distance from the road are displayed.

This functionality helps users visualize and analyze the proximity between schools and roads in rural areas, aiding in planning and improving accessibility.
